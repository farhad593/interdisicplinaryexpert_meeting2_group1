El Anatsui

Sculptural Installation & Mixed Media

About

El Anatsui is a Ghanaian sculptor active for much of his career in Nigeria. He has drawn particular international attention for his iconic "bottle-top installations," distinctive large-scale assemblages of thousands of pieces of aluminum sourced from alcohol recycling stations and sewn together with copper wire, which he arranges into metallic tapestries.

Education

  • College of Art, Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Ghana
  • Professor of Sculpture, University of Nigeria, Nsukka

Selected Exhibitions

  • 2023 - "El Anatsui: Meyina" - Prince Claus Fund Gallery, Amsterdam
  • 2019 - "El Anatsui: Triumphant Scale" - Haus der Kunst, Munich
  • 2015 - "El Anatsui: Five Decades" - El Museo del Barrio, New York
