Yayoi Kusama

Contemporary Installation Art

About

Yayoi Kusama is a Japanese contemporary artist who works primarily in sculpture and installation. She is known for her polka dot patterns and infinity installations, exploring themes of infinity, self-obliteration, and psychological trauma through her immersive art.

Education

  • Kyoto School of Arts and Crafts
  • Art Students League of New York

Selected Exhibitions

  • 2023 - "Yayoi Kusama: INFINITY MIRRORED ROOM" - Tate Modern, London
  • 2021 - "Cosmic Nature" - New York Botanical Garden
  • 2019 - "EVERY DAY I PRAY FOR LOVE" - David Zwirner, New York
