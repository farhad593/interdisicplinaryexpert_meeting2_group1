Takashi Murakami

Contemporary Pop Art & Superflat

About

Takashi Murakami is a Japanese contemporary artist known for blending fine art and pop culture. He is the founder of the Superflat movement, which combines traditional Japanese painting techniques with contemporary pop culture and fine art. His work encompasses painting, sculpture, fashion, merchandise, and animation.

Education

  • PhD in Nihonga, Tokyo University of the Arts
  • BFA in Nihonga, Tokyo University of the Arts

Selected Exhibitions

  • 2023 - "Takashi Murakami: Stepping on the Tail of a Rainbow" - The Broad, Los Angeles
  • 2019 - "MURAKAMI vs MURAKAMI" - Tai Kwun Contemporary, Hong Kong
  • 2017 - "The Octopus Eats Its Own Leg" - MCA Chicago
