Kehinde Wiley

Contemporary Portraiture

About

Kehinde Wiley is an American portrait painter known for his highly naturalistic paintings of African Americans in heroic poses. His work addresses the politics of representation by using the power of images to place contemporary urban people in the field of power.

Education

  • MFA, Yale University School of Art
  • BFA, San Francisco Art Institute

Selected Exhibitions

  • 2023 - "Kehinde Wiley: An Archaeology of Silence" - de Young Museum, San Francisco
  • 2022 - "Kehinde Wiley: The Prelude" - National Gallery, London
  • 2021 - "Kehinde Wiley: A New Republic" - The Brooklyn Museum
